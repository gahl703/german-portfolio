About Me

Who am I? I’m many things.


I’m creative. In my professional career, I enjoy discovering meaning in small details, whether through problem-solving or adapting to change. In my personal life, I express this creativity through world-building and crafting stories filled with twists and turns.


I’m driven. I take the initiative to tackle tasks, whether it’s answering a question I contemplated that day or engaging in a weeklong project. My determination ensures that I see every project through to completion.


I'm curious and often think beyond just the 'why' of any problem. Throughout my academic career, I’ve posed questions like “What are Makishi?” and “Why do we support teams?” These inquiries have challenged my analytical mindset.


That same mindset naturally led me to technology.


I enjoy working through structural problems, understanding how systems connect and turning ideas into something practical. Through my academic experiences and projects, I have developed a strong foundation in data, analysis, and systems thinking. I have even created a data-driven project that addressed questions I have been seeking to answer. This has allowed me to understand how I approach problems, which is through patience, curiosity, and clarity.


This website is a reflection of that balance.


I’m building a space where I can explore web development as a creative outlet, sharing projects that highlight my analytical work and upcoming projects that challenge my storytelling abilities.


Below, you can see what I have built and what I’m currently building.


I'm still early in my journey, but I hope you can join me on my road as a developer and a thinker.
