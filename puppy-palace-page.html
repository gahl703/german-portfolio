Puppy Palace System Analysis

James Madison University * Fall 2025 * Group Project (5 members)

Problem Statement

Currently, outdated manual bookkeeping methods to track customer information, creating risk of data error, reduced productivity and restrict scalability. To address this, the team analyzed the need for a web-based system capable of improving operational accuracy and efficiency.

My Role:

Business Analyst

What I did:

  • Assisted in document analysis to better understand Puppy Palace existing business operations
  • Mapped current workflow to construct the “As-is” Use Case diagram
  • Led requirement elicitation through structured stakeholder interview
  • Analyzed stakeholder feedback to develop the “To-be” Use Case diagram
  • Identified functional and non-functional requirements for the proposed web-based system

Key Outcomes

  • Identified key improvement areas involving reporting requirements, internal staff communication, and service scheduling
  • Proposed a more modernized and streamlined system workflow for Puppy Palace
  • Prepared business requirements and supporting diagrams for the system design phase to evaluate project feasibility and investment value
